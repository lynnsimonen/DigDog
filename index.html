Dig Dog

You are a dog that has buried all of your bones in the yard -- but forgotten where.
Try to dig them back up, but beware: the human gets angrier the more holes you make.
If you dig too much, he'll shoo you off the lawn and your precious bones will be lost!
The lawn is composed of a square grid of tiles.
Click on a square to dig in it and, if you're lucky, find a bone.
Digging a hole causes the human to get angrier, however,
and the danger meter will increase toward the point where the human chases you away.
